**Summation Notation:**
$$
\sum_{i=a}^{b} f(i)
$$

This represents the sum of the function  f(i)  evaluated at integer values of  i  from  a  to  b .

**Product Notation:**
$$
\prod_{i=a}^{b} f(i)
$$
  This represents the product of the function  f(i)  evaluated at integer values of  i  from  a  to  b .
